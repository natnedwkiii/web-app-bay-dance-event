On The One Event Co.

About Us

Established in Bay Area in 2023, On The One Event Co. is the central hub for dance enthusiasts in the electrifying San Francisco Bay Area. We understand that the heart of dance beats to its own rhythm, and we're here to ensure you never miss a beat. Our name, "On The One" takes inspiration from the rhythmic essence of funk music, where the emphasis on 'the one' sets the groove. Just as funk music inspires movement, our mission at On The One is to ensure you never miss a beat. Our name, "On The One," directly reflects the dance community's commitment to synchronizing movements with the infectious groove of funk. This ethos is at the heart of our mission - bringing together the entire Bay Area dance scene in one harmonious platform. Join us as we dance to the rhythm of unity!

Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to centralize and streamline the dance event experience in the Bay Area. We know that the dance community is vibrant, but sometimes fragmented. Dance battles, sessions, parties, and other events can be challenging to discover because organizers often rely on scattered Instagram posts. On The One Event Co. bridges this gap, making sure every dance event is just a click away.

Bay Area -
The Birth Place of Boogaloo, Strutting and Turfing

The San Francisco Bay Area has been instrumental in the development and popularization of dance styles such as boogaloo, strutting, and turfing. These unique and influential dance forms, born in the Bay Area, have had a profound impact on the global dance scene. The Bay Area's diverse cultural influences and a spirit of innovation have made it a hotbed of creative movement expression. Its dance history continues to inspire and influence dancers around the world.
